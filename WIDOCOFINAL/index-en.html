STOP Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

We created this ontology to actually allows us to express the differences between the concept album, "Non al denaro non all'amore né al cielo", by Fabrizio de André and the anthology, "Spoon River Anthology", by E. G. Masters. In fact, Fabrizio de André was inspired by the poems in his writing and through our work we hope to actually confront these two mediums to find similarities and differences.

Introduction back to ToC

STOP (Songs TO Poems) conceptualises the analysis of the differences and similarities between the concept album "Non all'amore non al denaro né al cielo" and the anthology, "Spoon River". The ontology can currently be used to reveal common keywords through the two different corpora of songs and poems, through the site (https://songstopoems.github.io/STOP/). We expect the ontology to be a starting point for further developments in the field of computational literary analysis.
